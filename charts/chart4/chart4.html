Welcome to the Exoplanet Playground!

Get ready to journey through the NASA Exoplanet Dataset, where we explore distant planets beyond our solar system.

This repository shares year wise discoveries from space missions and observatories, offering a unique peek into the diverse worlds out there.

Join us on this cosmic adventure as we uncover the mysteries and diversity of exoplanetary systems in our galaxy.

Guide
  1. View discovered planets and their respective discovery years at the top left corner of the visualization.
  2. Planet Information Tooltip: Hover over a planet to access detailed information in a tooltip. Details include planetary attributes and specifics about the discovery.
  3. Orbital Radius Representation: Proportional lines depict the orbital radius of each planet. Tooltip displays the orbital radius information upon hovering.
  4. Planet Size Representation: The circle size correlates to the planet's radius, calculated from the radius multiplier in combination with Earth or Jupiter's radius, depending on the data.
  5. Navigate by Year: Utilize the slider to explore exoplanet discoveries in specific years.
  6. Animation Feature: Engage the Play/Pause button to witness a chronological animation of exoplanet discoveries from 1992 to 2023.
Visualization Elements:

Marks: Line (Orbital Radius) and point (Planet)
Channels: Color: Hue(Planet Type) and Area (Planet Radius)
Attributes: Year, Planet Type, Planet Radius (calculated using Radius Multiplier and Comparative Planet), Orbital Radius
Interactions: Slider (Drag and Drop), Play/Pause (Temporal Visuals), Legends, Tooltips (Additional Inmformation)
